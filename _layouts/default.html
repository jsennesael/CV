Master's Thesis

The LLC converter is a DC/DC topology widely used in the industry but not yet in space applications. The topology is usually chosen for its high efficiency but the control of it is not obvious. The objective is to study the way to control the converter using the MSP430 microcontroller.

The above images show some basic simulations of the LLC circuit in Micro-Cap and it's gain curves in Python. This massive project is still a work-in-progress so more updates are to come!
